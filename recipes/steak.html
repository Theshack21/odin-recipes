Steak

Ingredients

  • Steak
  • Salt and pepper(or your spices of choice)
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Season steak and let sit in the fridge for a few hours
  2. Add butter to hot pan, then add steak
  3. cook just till colored on all sides, then turn down heat and add a small slice of more butter
  4. As the butter melts, use a spoon to scoop it up and drizzle it over the steak while its still cooking
  5. After a couple minutes of this, remove steak and let rest for 5-10 minutes
  6. Enjoy!
