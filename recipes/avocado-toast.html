Avocado Toast

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of sourdough bread
  • One whole avocado
  • Sea salt
  • Pepper
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Toast sourdough slices
  2. Drizzle olive oil over toasted slices
  3. Cut avocado in half, remove seed and scoop out one half and put it on each slice
  4. Mash avocado(preferrably with fork but really anything will suffice)
  5. Salt and pepper to taste
  6. Enjoy!
